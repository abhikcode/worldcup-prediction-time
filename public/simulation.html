How the engine works

A transparent, reproducible pipeline вАФ no black box.

01 ¬Ј Strength

Each team's strength is its official FIFA / Coca-Cola ranking points from the 1 April 2026 update вАФ the last before the tournament. FIFA's formula is Elo-based, so it already encodes squad quality and recent form across all 48 teams.

02 ¬Ј Match model

Every game is two independent Poisson goal draws. Expected goals are set by the rating gap, calibrated so an evenly-matched game draws about 26% of the time вАФ realistic for international football. Hosts get a modest home-crowd boost.

03 ¬Ј Structure

The real confirmed groups and the real fixed knockout bracket (R32 вЖТ R16 вЖТ QF вЖТ SF вЖТ Final). Group ties break on points вЖТ goal difference вЖТ goals scored; the eight best third-placed teams slot into FIFA's eligible Round-of-32 positions.

04 ¬Ј Knockouts

Drawn knockout games go to a penalty model вАФ a near coin-flip with a slight edge to the stronger side. The whole 104-match tournament is replayed 10,000 times and outcomes are tallied per team.

Reading the numbers. These are probabilities, not predictions. A 15% favourite still fails to win 85 times out of 100 вАФ the spread across the top tier is exactly the point. The model knows nothing about injuries, in-tournament form, tactics or red cards; it's a clean baseline built from ratings and structure.
